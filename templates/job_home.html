Job Description Based Candidate Pertinence Analysis

Given the large amount of job postings all over the web, applicants are left confused about the fact that whether their previous academic/work experience or skill-set aligns with the posted job or not. Identifying the abundance of skill & technology requirements proposed by the job recruiters and verifying our own eligibility could be a strenuous task. This system will eliminate this issue and will be helpful for identifying and recommending the right job for every applicant seeking the perfect job based on their portfolio.
